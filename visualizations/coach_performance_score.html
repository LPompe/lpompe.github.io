Manager performance score

Each dot is one manager. Score = their average (actual round − Elo-expected round) across every World Cup they managed — positive means they beat their teams' Elo-implied odds on average, negative means they fell short. The vertical spread has no meaning; it only keeps overlapping dots readable.
